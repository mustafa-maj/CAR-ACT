Our Story

Last year, I failed my driving test. After researching, I discovered that 75% of UK driving tests result in failure. Additionally, road accidents are currently at an all-time high due to human faults such as drunk driving, speeding, and poor reaction time. This made me realize that the traditional concept of human driving is flawed. Unlike humans, computers are not subject to these limitations, which is why I believe that autonomous cars are the future. However, there is skepticism and stigma surrounding this technology. To help people understand how self-driving technology works without any influence from biased media, I designed the CAR-ACT simulation. This simulation incorporates neural and deep learning used by many autonomous cars and sensor detection to replicate the behavior of modern self-driving cars.
